About Us

Bobby Erectors is a mechanical maintenance and industrial services company supporting site-based work, plant maintenance, skilled manpower deployment and contract operations.

The company supports welding, hot works, gas cutting, height works, chemical-area support, vehicle driving, fabrication assistance and general mechanical maintenance.

Our focus is to provide dependable manpower, safe work practices, proper documentation, payroll management and professional service support for industrial customers.
