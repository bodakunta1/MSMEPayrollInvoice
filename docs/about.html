About G Srinivasa Enterprises

G Srinivasa Enterprises is a mechanical maintenance and industrial services company supporting site-based work, plant maintenance, skilled manpower and contract operations.

The company provides support for welding, hot works, gas cutting, height works, chemical-area support, vehicle driving, fabrication assistance and general mechanical maintenance.

Our focus is to provide dependable manpower, safe working practices, proper documentation, payroll management and professional service support for industrial customers.
